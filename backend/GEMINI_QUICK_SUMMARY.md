# Quick Answer: Gemini vs OpenAI for TapUp

## Should You Switch? **YES! ✅**

### Cost Savings: **33% cheaper**
- OpenAI: $0.15/$0.60 per million tokens
- Gemini Flash: $0.10/$0.40 per million tokens
- **Savings: ~$0.65 per 1,000 requests**

### Features: **Better for your use case**
- ✅ Same text generation quality
- ✅ 8x larger context window (1M vs 128k tokens)
- ✅ Faster responses
- ✅ Better for video (multimodal support)
- ✅ Generous free tier

### Migration: **Easy**
- Similar API structure
- Just swap the client library
- ~2-3 hours to migrate all files

### Recommendation
**Switch to Gemini 2.0 Flash** - You'll save money and get better performance for video content!

See `MIGRATE_TO_GEMINI.md` for step-by-step guide.
